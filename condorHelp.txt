To submit a job:
----------------

condor_submit condorTemplate.submit


Troubleshooting:

 condor_q -better-analyze JOBID


History:
--------

condor_history mauri

for details:

condor_history -long mauri

condor_q - Lists the jobs in the queue. Can be invoked with your username:

condor_q mauri
